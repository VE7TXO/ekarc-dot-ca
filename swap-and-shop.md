# Swap and Shop

Swap and Shop is now being maintained as a Google Docs document. Please contact swapandshop@ekarc.ca to have your items listed!

**LAST UPDATED January 30, 2026**

---

## Welcome

Please contact buyers and sellers directly. All items will be listed for four weeks. They can be re-listed for a further four weeks upon request. This listing is updated weekly, usually on Monday. Additions or deletions can be made via email to swapandshop@ekarc.ca

Please be sure to include your call sign, your email address, telephone number and area code. Also please know when you buy or sell something on this list, so we can keep it up to date. These listings may also be shared during our 2 meter net on the 3rd Thursday of every month.
